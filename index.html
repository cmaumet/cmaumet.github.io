Welcome

Hi! I'm Camille, a research scientist in neuroinformatics at the Empenn team, Inria Rennes Bretagne Atlantique / IRISA.

I study neuroimaging reproducibility. My current research focuses on the variability of analytical pipelines and its impact on our ability to reuse (and use) brain imaging datasets. I am also an open science advocate and participate regularly in international communities such as Brainhack, the INCF, and OHBM Open Science SIG.
