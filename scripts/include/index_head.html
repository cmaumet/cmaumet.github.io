Welcome

Hi! I'm Camille Maumet, a research scientist in neuroinformatics in the Empenn team at the Inria Centre at Rennes Uni. / IRISA.

I study neuroimaging reproducibility. My current research focuses on the variability of analytical pipelines and its impact on our ability to (re)use brain imaging datasets.

I am also an open science advocate. I currently serve as a member of the Brain Imaging Data Structure (BIDS) steering group. I am an alumni of INCF Data Sharing task force, OHBM Open Science SIG and of the French Committee for Open Science, I have also actively taken part in the Brainhack community.
